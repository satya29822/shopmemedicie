      ~_~
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      At Nobi's house, Nobi was talking about a contest...
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Nobi: Tomorrow, there will be a contest at school, whoever gets the most beautiful play will win. In my group are Giant, Suneo, and Shizuka. In a few moments, they will be here.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: Good luck!
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Nobi: (begging for Doraemon) Please give me a gadget that will help us.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: But, that's a contest in school, right?
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Nobi: I really wanted to win. Maybe some of your gadgets will be useful at the play.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon placed a big gadget, like a printer, and above it was bowl-shaped that has been turned around, with tentacles around it, beside it was a row full of cassette tapes. (Note: Sorry, I don't really know how to explain such things.)
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: Auto Action Prompter and Script Cassette!
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Then, he placed out a gadget again, looking like controls. The gadget had 8 levers, 4 very long ones, and in front of it was 4 ordinary buttons. Behind the long levers, 2 screens beside each other. And at the back of the set, were 3 lights, 2 were big, and at the center was a small one.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: All-Around Set!
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Nobi examined the gadgets, which he looked like he's not sure.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: You're play will be good and beautiful because of those.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Downstairs, his mom called Nobi.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Mom: Nobita!
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Nobi: (running happily ) They're here!
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      The 3 of them were in Nobi's room, arguing about what the play are they going to do.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Giant: Let's do the play called "Superman!" I'll be the main character!
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Suneo: (complaining) We don't have a costume for that, our play should be about Shakespeare's play called "Hamlet".
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Shizuka: That play is not for Elementary, the play should be "The Moon Princess."
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      While they're arguing, Nobi and Doraemon was just looking at them...
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: (saying to Nobi) Oh no!
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      So Doraemon said to them...
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: You guys should choose what character you wanted to be in the play.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Suneo: The play should be more better if there was a prince.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Shizuka: I should be the princess.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Giant: I wanted something, just give me some character that has some kind of supernatural power.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: (depressed) We should find a good play for you guys.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Suneo: We should make a very cool, like international play so the teachers would be impressed.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Nobi: (complaining) That's not good, the play should be easy, like for kids only.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: Give a play that will relate to people just like Nobi.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      So Doraemon placed something on Nobi's head.
  
    +
  
  


      
                          
      Doraemon: If Nobi can understand it, you guys can also understand it.
      •••••••••••••••••••••••••••••
      Doraemon: The easiest script that can be played.
  
    +

      From the tiny screen beside the upside down-bowl of the Auto Action Prompter, a 'ting' sounded. And appearing some saying, "B-013"
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon searched at the row of the cassette tapes, looking for the same CT that was written, "B-013"
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: (very excited) Here it is! B-013! This play had a prince, a princess, a person that had supernatural power, and the story that Nobi wanted.
  
    +
  
  


      

      All: (impressed) Wow!
  
    +
  
  


      

      Shizuka: There should be a script so we can memorize the dialogue.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: (smiling) We don't need that.
  
    +
  
  


      

      So he explained the contents...
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: I'll just set this and you will all automatically move and talk based on the roles of your play inside this cassette tape.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Giant: That's good.
  
    +
  
  


      

      So Doraemon placed the cassette tape inside the Auto Action Prompter with a 'click'.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: Then, connect it to the all around set.
  
    +
  
  


      

      The All Around Set began to sparkle, showing a scene that mostly covered the half of Nobi's room.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: First scene.
  
    +
  
  


      

      The scene was a castle from a distance at the very top of the mountain, the moon shines brightly near it. Under it, was waves splashing near the camera. Nobi and his friends was very surprised when they saw the scene.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: You also have sound effects.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Shizuka: (shocked) It's beautiful!
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: Your costumes will also be from the magic rays.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon hovered the light at Suneo, making a 'poof' sound and covering a fog around Suneo. Then Suneo was now wearing a 'tiny' prince costume.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Suneo: (excited) Wow! I'm a prince!
  
    +
  
  


      

      Then, Doraemon hovered it at Giant, and his costume was a very dark cloth that covered most of his body, as his head was covered by a darker cloth, like a witch. And he was holding a wooden curved staff.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Giant: (confused) What's this?
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: (looking at the description) The witch that has supernatural powers was written in this story.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Then, Doraemon hovered it at Nobi, and his costume was a 'very pretty' princess.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: You're the princess on the other realm.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Nobi: (shocked) I don't like this!!
  
    +
  
  


      

      Then, he hovered it a Shizuka, and his costume was a mermaid.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: And Shizuka will be the 'Princess Mermaid.'
  
    +
  
  


      

      The 3 Boys: (completely surprised) WOW!
  
    +
  
  


      

      Shizuka: (covering her 'boobies') It's like I'm not wearing anything, It's embarrassing!!!
  
    +
  
  


      

      3 Boys: You're not really embarrassing! You're really, really beautiful! It's a very good masterpiece!
  
    +
  
  


      

      So for the first scene, Shizuka, wearing the mermaid costume, went in.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: If the set has already started, you will automatically move and talk.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Shizuka went to a nearby rock, and started singing...
  
    +
  
  


      

      Shizuka: (singing) Good Evening Everyone, the moon is bright and the stars are bright...
      ••••••••••••••••••••••••
      Shizuka: (thought) Wow, I can sing very well.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Suneo: (surprised by Shizuka's voice) It looks like a realistic musical show.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Nobi: (also surprised by her voice) Shizuka is really good.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Giant: (also surprised) She can sing better than me.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Doraemon: On the second scene, Suneo will appear.
  
    +
  
  


      

      Suneo went into the scene. But the scene changed, appearing a ladder beside a pirate ship. Suneo climbed up the ladder that goes in the ship...
  
    +
  
  


      

      Suneo: It's look really cool since the ship, which is our prop, looks really real.
  
    +
  
  


      

      But when Suneo went into the ship, the ship started rocking and shaking, and around him, the waves were pushing the ship, rain poured from above. It's a total disaster...
  
    +
  
  


      

      Suneo: (shouting) WAAAHH!!!!! HELPPPP!!!
  
    +
  
  


      

      Giant and Nobi: The special effects are really cool.
